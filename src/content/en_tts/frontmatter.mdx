Plain Dharma. Six foundational Buddhist teachings, in plain modern English. By Alex Miller.

These are six of the Buddha's foundational teachings — the first talk, not-self, the fire sermon, loving-kindness, the foundations of mindfulness, and how to decide what to believe — rendered fresh for a first-time listener. This is not a scholarly translation, but a plain reading: an attempt to make these teachings clear without losing their substance.

It is released into the public domain under Creative Commons Zero — free to copy, share, and read aloud, with no permission needed, in keeping with the tradition of freely given dharma.
